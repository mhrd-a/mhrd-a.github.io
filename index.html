This research examines whether changes in local employment conditions influence entrepreneurial activity on online crowdfunding platforms such as Kickstarter. Specifically, whether strengthening or weakening labor markets affect the number of new crowdfunding projects launched in each state and the likelihood that those projects reach their funding goals.
